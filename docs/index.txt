Plugins
=======

Each plugin has its own page with details like functionalities and requirements.

* :doc:`onewire	</plugins/onewire/index>` Use 1 Wire devices
* :doc:`cidmodem </plugins/cidmodem/index>` Get caller ID with a modem
* :doc:`ipx800 </plugins/ipx800/index>` Use IPX800 relayboard
* :doc:`mirror </plugins/mirror/index>` Use of Mirror by Violet
* :doc:`plcbus </plugins/plcbus/index>` Use Plcbus devices
* :doc:`teleinfo </plugins/teleinfo/index>` Get electricity consumption information (only French providers)
* :doc:`wol_ping </plugins/wol_ping/index>` Ping and wake up computers with Wake on Lan
* :doc:`x10_heyu </plugins/x10_heyu/index>` Use X10 devices with Heyu
* :doc:`xpl_time </plugins/xpl_time/index>` Send date and time over xPL
